Welcome to the Raj Lab!


From Blood to Brain

The Raj Laboratory is dedicated to understanding the molecular mechanisms of neurodegenerative disorders, including Alzheimer’s disease, Parkinson’s disease, and Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis. To achieve these goals, we leverage the fields of genomics, computational biology, neuroscience, and immunology coupled with basic cell and molecular biology to obtain a systems level understanding of disease. We are a hybrid lab with a strong computational biology that is mainly focused on performing data analyses and an experimental side, where wet-lab experiments are performed.
